# Growth Marketing Flow

A curated growth-marketing Flow: seven hand-picked marketing skills wired into one
funnel-shaped router. It takes you from "who is this for and what do they want" all
the way to "did the change actually move the number," firing the right skill for the
job and refusing to call a result a win until it is measured.

## What it covers

- **Research**: mine customers and competitors for the real language and pains (`customer-research`).
- **Copy**: turn that language into clear, converting page copy (`copywriting`).
- **Acquisition (organic)**: diagnose and fix what blocks search traffic (`seo-audit`).
- **Acquisition (outbound)**: write cold email that actually gets replies (`cold-email`).
- **Launch**: plan an announcement that builds momentum (`launch`).
- **Optimize**: find and fix the conversion blockers on a page (`cro`).
- **Validate**: design a statistically honest experiment before claiming a win (`ab-testing`).

The skills are vendored from Corey Haines (MIT). The routing layer is by Flowy. See
`ATTRIBUTION.md`. Listed for attribution; Corey Haines does not endorse Flowy.

## How to use

Run `/flowy growth-marketing-flow`. The router (`FLOW.md`) then fires the right skill
for each intent and holds you to a gate, a named artifact, at every step. You do not
paste anything into your own config; the Flowy plugin installs the routing for you.
